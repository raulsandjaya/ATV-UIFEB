This Year's Grand Theme

The Transformative Auditor

Strengthening Assurance Amidst Evolving Business Challenges.

Empowering future auditors to be resilient, forward-thinking professionals — equipped to face the challenges of modern assurance with confidence and integrity.

In today's evolving business environment, auditors are increasingly required to adapt their approaches to meet new and emerging challenges. The advancement of digital technologies, heightened regulatory demands, and stronger expectations for transparency and accountability have prompted the audit profession to refine its methods while maintaining its core responsibilities in assurance.

Through this year's grand theme, the 25th ATV seeks to highlight the strategic contribution of auditors in upholding trust, managing risk, and enhancing assurance quality.

What We Bring

Our Events

Four flagship programs. One transformative season. Pick your arena.

01 — COMPETE

Audit Competition

Audit Competition will bring participants from across the nation to compete in various rounds, including written tests, quizzes, and presentations — designed to test their audit knowledge and teamwork.

02 — SOLVE

Audit Case Challenge

An exciting opportunity for participants across Indonesia to work on audit cases that require audit skills and creative thinking — navigating Paper Writing, Audit Case Pitching, and the Audit Case Forum.

03 — SHARPEN

Training

Aligned with "Stepping Up to Practice: Navigating Risk, Adjustments, and Judgment in Audit Execution," this training builds essential auditing skills hands-on — risk identification, adjustment entries, and professional judgment in one cohesive framework.

04 — LEARN

Workshop

The 25th ATV's Workshop provides comprehensive insights on the entire flow of the competition — benefits and tips on joining Audit Competition & Audit Case Challenge, given by previous judges and winners of last year's ATV.

JisuLife

JisuLife

Founded in 2016, JisuLife is dedicated to consistently pushing the boundaries of high-performance, energy-efficient fan technology. Guided by the brand values of Care, Comfort, Convenience, Collaboration, and Creativity, JisuLife has always placed innovation at its core — portable cooling solutions designed to redefine comfort in every outdoor experience, from adventurous getaways to daily commutes.

Specializing in portable fans — from handheld and neck fans to table, outdoor, and stroller fans — JisuLife blends minimalist aesthetics, user-centric design, and eco-conscious materials to deliver ergonomic comfort and lasting value.

JUVAby zap

Juva by ZAP

Juva by ZAP is a skincare brand developed by ZAP Clinic, a beauty and aesthetic service provider in Indonesia. Introduced as part of ZAP's expansion into skincare, it offers serums, sunscreen, toner, cleansers, and body care products that complement professional aesthetic treatments — built on clinically tested formulations and active ingredients supporting skin health.

Distributed through ZAP Clinic outlets and official online marketplaces, Juva by ZAP leverages ZAP's aesthetic research to provide skincare that is safe, effective, and suitable for everyday use.
